Click the button — the new theme wipes in as a circle expanding from wherever your cursor was.

Uses the View Transitions API (document.startViewTransition): the theme class flips inside the transition callback, then a manual clip-path animation is played on the incoming ::view-transition-new(root) pseudo-element, sized to reach the farthest corner from the click point. Browsers without support (Firefox, older Safari) just snap instantly — no polyfill needed.
